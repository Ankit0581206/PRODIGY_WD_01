Food Menu

Dum Biryani

A classic Indian rice dish cooked with aromatic spices, succulent meat (chicken, lamb, or vegetarian), and fragrant basmati rice. The meat and rice are layered and slow-cooked on dum (steamed over low heat), allowing the flavors to meld together perfectly. Each bite is a burst of rich, well-balanced spices and tender, juicy meat.

Price: ₹ 550

Chicken Tandoori Biryani

A delectable fusion of two iconic Indian dishes â€“ tandoori chicken and biryani. Marinated chicken pieces are first cooked in a tandoor (clay oven) until smoky and tender, then layered with fragrant basmati rice, aromatic spices, and yogurt. The result is a flavorful and aromatic biryani with the smoky essence of tandoori chicken.

Price: ₹ 450

Chicken Tandoori

Chicken marinated in a rich blend of yogurt, spices like cayenne, cumin, and garam masala, and lemon juice. The marinated chicken is then cooked in a tandoor (clay oven) until charred and smoky on the outside, while remaining succulent and tender on the inside. A classic Indian dish, bursting with flavors.

Price: ₹ 250

Lemon Tandoori

Similar to the classic Chicken Tandoori, but with a tangy twist. Chicken pieces are marinated in a yogurt-based blend with lemon juice, zest, and a host of aromatic spices. The lemon adds a bright, citrusy note to the marinade, perfectly complementing the smoky flavors from the tandoor.

Price: ₹ 350

Classic Shwarma

Thinly sliced marinated meat (chicken, beef, or lamb) stacked on a vertical rotisserie and slowly roasted, creating a crispy exterior and tender, juicy interior. Served wrapped in flatbread with fresh vegetables like tomatoes, onions, and pickled turnips, and a tangy garlic sauce or tahini.

Price: ₹ 120

Spicy Shwarma

A fiery twist on the classic shawarma, where the meat is marinated in a blend of hot spices like chili peppers, cumin, paprika, and cayenne. The marinated meat is then roasted on a vertical rotisserie, creating a crispy and spicy exterior while remaining tender inside. Served with fresh veggies and a cooling sauce to balance the heat.

Price: ₹ 180
